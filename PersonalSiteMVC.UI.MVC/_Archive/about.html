Every day presented its own unique set of challenges and problems that I had to resolve at a moment's notice. It also taught me the value of great teamwork, managing both front of house and back of house employees in tandem. I received my Associate’s in Computer Animation from Full Sail University, so I’ve always had a passion for technology. I’m intrigued by the endless possibilities that technology offers, and I’m motivated to be a part of its future. Since COVID-19 has crippled the hospitality industry, I thought that now was the best time to take that passion and make it my career.
